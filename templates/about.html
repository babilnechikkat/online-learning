About Us

iguard talent mentors is an online mentoring program for developing talents in Computer world

Its unprecedented combination of offerings, all free of charge, includes:

  • One-on-one mentoring by international experts.
  • Tailored learning pathways for optimal individual talent development.
  • Long-term support over several years of accruing expertise.

Currently,iguard talent is recruiting partners in order to engage the most outstanding students and experts as mentees and mentors, respectively
